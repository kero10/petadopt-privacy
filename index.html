Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Welcome to PetAdopt. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data. By using PetAdopt, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Full name and email address when you create an account
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Short bio (optional)
  • Pet listing details: name, species, breed, age, photos, description, and location
  • Adoption application details you submit
  • Shelter name and location information (for Shelter Admins)

We also collect limited technical information automatically:

  • Device push notification token (for in-app notifications, with your permission)
  • App usage data for debugging and performance (no personal identifiers)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your PetAdopt account
  • Display pet listings and match adopters with pets
  • Process and manage adoption applications
  • Send you in-app and push notifications about your applications and listings
  • Enable communication between pet owners, shelter admins, and adopters
  • Improve and maintain the app
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent misuse

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising targeting.

3. Information Shared With Others

Certain information is visible to other users as part of normal functionality:

  • Your name and profile photo appear on your public profile and on pet listings you create
  • Pet listing details (photos, description, location city) are visible to all users browsing the app
  • Shelter name and location are visible on the public shelter profile page
  • Adoption application details are shared only with the pet owner or shelter admin managing that listing

We do not share your email address, phone number, or exact address publicly.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase, protected by industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is restricted by Row-Level Security policies.

Despite our security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your credentials private.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information and pet listings will be removed within 30 days, except where required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@petadopt.app.

8. Children's Privacy

PetAdopt is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Push Notifications

We request permission to send push notifications to keep you informed about application updates and listing activity. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device Settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of PetAdopt after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
